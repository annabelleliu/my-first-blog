About Me

I am a rising Senior at Rye Country Day School this year. My birthday is September 23rd, and I will be turning 18. I was born in Hong Kong and lived in Beijing until I was 7, where I first attended Western Academy of Beijing and later the International School of Beijing. I now live in Greenwich, CT with my 3 siblings. I have a 20 year old, 9 year old brother and 7 year old sister. This summer, on top of working as a part-time hostess at a French restaurant in Old Greenwich, I am participating in Girls Who Code: Microsoft and having a great time!
